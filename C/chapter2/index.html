Introduction

C suppourts number of built-in operators. Some of them are + , - , * , & and many more.. C operators are mainly divided into 8 types, which are:

  1. Arthemetic Operators
  2. Relational Operators
  3. Logical Operators
  4. Assignment Operators
  5. Increment and decrement Operators
  6. Conditional Operators
  7. Bitwise Operators
  8. Special Operators
