Head of DBMS in S5AMP receives formal warning and notice to submit company data!


The Chairman of S5AMP, Ansh Sarkar, has issued a formal warning to the Head of DBMS, Akshat Kesharwani, for not meeting the
performance expectations set out for his role. The warning comes as a result of repeated instances where Kesharwani's work
has been found to be below standard and his team has reported dissatisfaction with his management style.

Kesharwani has been given two weeks to improve his work performance, failing which the company will be forced to take further
action. Additionally, he has been instructed to collect all the data of S5AMP and make a file, which must be submitted to
Chairman Ansh Sarkar within the same two-week timeframe.

This notice to submit company data is a mandatory requirement, and failure to comply may result in further disciplinary action,
including termination of employment. The company has emphasized that it takes data security seriously and expects Kesharwani to
handle the data file with the utmost care.

"We value our employees and their contributions to the company, but we cannot compromise on the quality of work and management. We
have issued this warning to Mr. Kesharwani with the hope that he will take the necessary steps to improve his performance and meet
the expectations of his role," said Chairman Ansh Sarkar.

The Chairman has also assured that the company will provide Kesharwani with the support and resources necessary to improve his
performance, and hopes that he will take this warning in a positive light.

This warning to the Head of DBMS in S5AMP highlights the company's commitment to maintaining a high standard of work and
management, and underscores the importance of accountability and data security in today's business world.
